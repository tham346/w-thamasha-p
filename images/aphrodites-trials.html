Project Overview

Developed a dynamic narrative for the Greek myth of Psyche and Cupid, using Figma to create graphics, animations, and layouts that enhanced the storytelling experience. The goal was to visually represent the themes of love, betrayal, and transformation, immersing the audience in the unfolding narrative.

Design Approach

Focused on conveying the myth’s emotional depth and themes by creating expressive visuals in Figma. A cohesive visual style was developed to captivate the audience, ensuring that the design highlighted key moments and emotions from the story.

Key Features
Graphics & Animations: Created in Figma, the visuals brought the myth’s scenes to life, synchronized with the unfolding narrative.
‍Emotional Storytelling: Visual elements that reflected the intense emotions of love, betrayal, and growth in the characters of Psyche and Cupid.
‍Cohesive Visual Style: Consistent colour palettes, typography, and layouts to unify the project and create an immersive experience.
